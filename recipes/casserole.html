King Ranch Chicken Casserole

This classic King Ranch chicken casserole is bursting with chicken, corn tortillas, tomatoes, peppers, and cheesy goodness. From what I hear, it's impossible to go to any sort of potluck in Texas and not see one of these.
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
10-15 servings
30 minutes
INGREDIENTS
  • 1 onion
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 1 chicken soup
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 4 tbps of sour cream
  • green chile peppers
  • homemade chicken broth
  • cumin
  • chili powder
  • dried oregano
  • corn tortillas
  • shredded cheese
  • cooked chicken
DIRECTIONS
  1. Sauté the onion, red bell pepper, and green bell pepper until they're warmed through.
  2. Stir together the sautéed vegetables, canned soups, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, sour cream, and spices in a large bowl until the ingredients are well-combined.
  3. Spread a few tablespoons of the sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Top the sauce with about half the chicken. Spread half the sauce over the chicken, then top with ⅓ of the cheese. Arrange a layer of tortillas over the cheese.
  4. Add remaining chicken, then top with the remaining sauce (reserving ½ cup). Top with ½ of the remaining cheese and remaining tortillas, then with the reserved sauce, and the remaining cheese.
  5. Add remaining chicken, then top with the remaining sauce (reserving ½ cup). Top with ½ of the remaining cheese and remaining tortillas, then with the reserved sauce, and the remaining cheese.
